Travel and Transport

“Every mountain top is within reach if you just keep climbing.”

-Barry Finlay, Kilimanjaro and Beyond

Driving:

From Denver by way of eastern Grand County

US Highway 40 from I-70 west, cross the Continental Divide over Berthoud Pass to enter through Winter Park and Fraser Valley.

From Denver by way of western Grand County

I-70 west to Silverthorne, go 38 miles north on Highway 9 to enter through Kremmling.

Greyhound and Amtrack:

There are options to get to Grandby, CO (less than 30 min drive to Grand Lake) from Denver via Greyhound and Amtrack. We suggest you visit the "Visit Grand County" website to explore these options here

Shuttle Service from DIA:

A private, regularly scheduled shuttle is available from Denver to Winter Park (45 min drive from Grand Lake). You can find out more details here

Taxi Service in Grand County:

Valley Taxi is is a local taxi service in Winter Park, Fraser and throughout Grand County. It is recommended you make reservations at least 24 hours in advance. You can find out more information here

Uber and Lyft:

Uber and Lyft are in short supply in Grand County. If you plan to drink, we suggest you find lodging near the shuttle pick drop off location, or call Valley Taxi to arrange a taxi in advance.
